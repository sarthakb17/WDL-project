Information of the movie :

Aniket, unhappily married to Avani, devises a sinister plan with his girlfriend Devika to fake a suicide pact and murder Avani, who suffers from a severe mental disorder and resists divorce. However, their scheme unravels when Avani's body and suicide note vanish after her death. Dr. Anshuman, Avani's psychiatrist and secret lover, reveals he killed her to protect his reputation after discovering she was pregnant with his child. As the trio attempts to dispose of the body, they inadvertently commit more murders, including killing a woman and a police inspector in chaotic circumstances. Planning to bury the bodies later, they store them at Aniket's house but meet a darkly ironic end when a falling chandelier crushes them all. The story concludes with termites gnawing on a nylon rope, symbolizing the ultimate poetic justice.
