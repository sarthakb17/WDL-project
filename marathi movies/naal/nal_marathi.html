Information of the movie :

Naal (2018) tells the story of Chaitanya, an eight-year-old boy living with his father, mother, and grandmother in a remote Maharashtra village. He learns from his uncle that his mother is not his biological parent, leading him to believe his "real" mother is someone named Parvati. Chaitanya tests his mother's love by pretending to run away but is beaten when he returns, deepening his emotional distance from her. He causes his grandmother's injury in an attempt to delay a trip to meet Parvati, but the calf they own dies, further affecting the family. Chaitanya's grandmother dies during a trip to the city, and Chaitanya becomes upset, unaware of the situation. He later realizes that the mother who raised him is the one who truly cares for him. By the end, Chaitanya calls his mother "Aai" again, acknowledging her love and presence in his life. The film explores themes of love, identity, and family bonds.
